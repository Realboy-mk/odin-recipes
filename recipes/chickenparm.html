Chicken Parmesean

Pan fried chicken breast topped with melty cheese!

This comfort classic is everything you want in a restaurant quality home cooked meal. Chicken Parmesean is a staple of Italian restaurants all over the world and for good reason. Follow our recipe!

Ingredients

Marinara Sauce

  • 3 Tbsp, extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 large sprigs basil
  • Kosher salt

Chicken

  • 1 c. Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 c. finely greated parmsean
  • 3/4 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 boneless, skinless chicken cutlets
  • 1 c. shredded mozzarella
  • ground bleck pepper, to taste
